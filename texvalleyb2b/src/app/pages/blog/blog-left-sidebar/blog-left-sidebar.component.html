HOW TRADITIONAL BUSINESS WORKS? 

In Traditional Business, the buyers come directly to the shops and purchase the products they need.

They have to spend much time for purchasing different models.

The Buyer will purchase the product through direct visit and the transaction made immediately where the single buyer can meet individual supplier and individual brand

 

CREDIT SERVICE IN TRADITIONAL BUSINESS 

In case the buyer needs credit-based service, it depends on the supplier to evaluate and provide credit as per supplier terms which will be stated in MOU to the buyer. After the completion of mutual understanding process between both of them, business will take place. The unknown buyers can’t get the benefit of credit service through suppliers immediately through Traditional Business process, this is how the traditional business takes place between buyer and supplier 

 

HOW TEXVALLEY B2B BUSINESS WORKS? 

The Texvalley B2B works through E-Commerce Platform where a single buyer can able to find N number of suppliers at a time.

The Texvalley B2B will interlink the two unknown strangers into business in single platform with multiple Brands.
