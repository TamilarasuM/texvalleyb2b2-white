REPLACEMENT POLICY

TVB2B will accept replacement of products within 3 days from date of receipt; only in case there is a significant difference in the products ordered from the product supplied (wrong colour, size related issues, quality issues or product is damaged) and where the Product and the Packaging is in its original condition (should be unused, unaltered, unsoiled). 

TVB2B however cannot accept the replacement in case:

  • The product has been used or altered
  • There is any physical damage to the product. 

To replace a product, please raise a replacement request via email at replacements@texvalleyb2b.in by providing the following details:

  • Order ID
  • Images clearly demonstrating the problem/ issue

Post receiving your request Customer Support will confirm the return request and will inform you about the pickup process. Please ensure that product is in unused and original condition, include all labels, original packing and invoice along with the product. TVB2B shall not be responsible for any items sent to us for replacement without our confirmation.

 

COST OF REPLACEMENT:

  • TVB2B will bear the cost of replacement of the product provided the replacement request has been accepted by TVB2B.
  • TVB2B shall not be responsible for any items sent to us for replacement without our confirmation and will not bearing the cost for such cases.
